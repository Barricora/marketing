Questions

Frequently asked questions.

Everything teams usually need to know before trying Barricora.

Does Barricora work on phones and tablets?+

Yes. Barricora is designed as a mobile-first web application and also works on desktop computers.

Can we use our own audit questions?+

Business plans can include custom audit libraries. Standard plans include Barricora's focused construction safety audit categories.

Can actions be closed with photo evidence?+

Yes. Owners can update corrective actions, add close-out notes and attach evidence before an action is marked complete.

Can reports include our company logo?+

Company branding is planned for Professional and Business plans, including logos on generated reports.

Do users need to install an app?+

No installation is required. Users open Barricora securely in their browser and can save it to their phone's home screen.
